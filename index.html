Our Framework

Our practice is built on a framework designed to shift power back to the creator, the collector, and the community.

DE-CENTRALIZED

We believe archives shouldn't just belong to massive data centers, old institutions or galleries. We bring professional cataloging directly to your space, beyond institutional gatekeeping.

DATA-DRIVEN

We enable artists, collectors and communities to own, control, and utilize their data. No third-party dependencies--just your collection carefully mapped and in your hands.

DYNAMIC

Accession records and archives are inherently political; they shape how art and collections are understood. We reject the idea of a fixed archive aligned with colonial and extractive standards. We build agile systems that are responsive to changing cultural realities and fluid narratives.

Our Process

We operate under a rigorous, transparent workflow designed to guarantee data integrity and physical safety for every object handled.

01

Physical Verification

Cross-referencing objects against existing manifests to trace provenance.

02

Condition Check

Meticulous material examination, structural assessment, and scale mapping.

03

Conservation Treatment -- Conditional

Stabilization and preservation measures--executed only when structurally required.

04

High-Resolution Photography

Visual documentation including high-fidelity macro details and true-color states.

05

Systematic Cataloging

Deploying custom archival tagging and physical location tracking mechanics.

06

Database Synchronization

Encoding verified data and physical storage points into digital records.

07

Final Validation

Integrity checking to guarantee high data accuracy before permanent recording.
