Our Framework

Our practice is built on a framework designed to shift power back to the creator, the collector, and the community.

DE-CENTRALIZED

We believe archiving shouldn't just belong to massive institutions or galleries.

We bring professional cataloging directly to your space, beyond institutional gatekeeping.

DATA-DRIVEN

We enable artists, collectors and small communities to own, control, and utilize their own data.

No third-party dependencies--just your collection carefully mapped and in your hands.

DYNAMIC

Accession records and archives are inherently political; they shape how collections are understood.

We reject the idea of a fixed archive aligned with colonial standards.

We build agile digital systems that are responsive to changing cultural realities and fluid narratives.
